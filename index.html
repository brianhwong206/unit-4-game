Crystal Collector Game (steamed bun version)

You arrive hungry at your local neighborhood restaurant with a craving for steamed buns and a limited amount of cash.

There are four images of buns pictured below. By clicking on a bun, you will add the value of that bun to your bill.

You win by matching your total bill to the random amount of cash you have arrived with.

You lose if your bill exceeds the amount in your wallet.

The value of each bun is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each round will randomize the value of each bun.
